## What APMG Services Does

APMG Services provides complete property maintenance support across eight main service categories.

## Main Service Categories

1. Electrical Services

APMG Services provides electrical repair, maintenance, installation and support services for commercial, industrial and residential properties. This may include general electrical works, lighting, power points, switchboard related support, fault finding, safety related works, upgrades and ongoing electrical maintenance.

2. Plumbing Services

APMG Services provides plumbing maintenance and repair solutions across a wide range of property types. This may include general plumbing repairs, leaks, blocked drains, tapware, toilets, fixtures, pipe related issues, hot water related works and reactive plumbing support.

3. Carpentry and Joinery

APMG Services provides carpentry and joinery support for repairs, upgrades, installations and maintenance works. This may include doors, locks, frames, cabinetry, shelving, timber repairs, fit outs, minor structural repairs and general carpentry requirements.

4. Painting Services

APMG Services started with painting and continues to provide professional painting solutions for commercial, industrial, education, aged care, childcare, residential and body corporate properties. This includes internal painting, external painting, repainting, touch ups, repairs, maintenance painting and project painting.

5. Handyman Services

APMG Services provides general handyman support for small repairs, maintenance tasks and property improvement works. This is ideal for clients who need reliable support across day to day property issues without having to source multiple trades.

6. General Property Make Safe Services

APMG Services provides urgent make safe support to help secure and protect properties after damage, faults, hazards, incidents or emergency situations. The focus is to make the site safe, reduce risk and support the next steps for repair or reinstatement.

7. Gardening and Landscaping Services

APMG Services provides gardening, grounds maintenance and landscaping support for commercial, industrial, residential, body corporate, education, childcare and aged care sites. This may include lawn care, garden maintenance, clean ups, soft landscaping, grounds presentation and ongoing outdoor maintenance.

8. Flooring Services

APMG Services provides flooring repair, replacement and maintenance support. This may include timber, vinyl, laminate, carpet related works, floor repairs, replacement, preparation and general flooring solutions depending on the site requirement.
