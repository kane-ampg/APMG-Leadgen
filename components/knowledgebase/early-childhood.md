# APMG (Australian Property Maintenance Group) — Early Childhood / Early Learning Knowledge Base

APMG Services (Australian Property Maintenance Group) is a multi-trade property maintenance company that positions itself as a single, reliable partner for all trade work at early childhood development centres and early learning environments. For this sector it delivers scheduled maintenance, urgent repairs, and full-scale upgrades/fit-outs — spanning painting, carpentry, flooring, electrical, plumbing, grounds, handyman, and make-safe — all built around safety, cleanliness, compliance, and minimal disruption to children, educators, and families. APMG is based at Chirnside Park, Victoria, and its work for this sector is tailored specifically to the routines, brand standards, and regulatory requirements of learning and care settings.

**Note on company scope:** early childhood / early learning is a specialisation APMG markets to through this portfolio — it is **not** APMG's only market. APMG's mission statement describes the company as providing end-to-end trade solutions for **"industrial, commercial, and residential clients."** Present early learning as a focus area and specialisation, not as APMG's exclusive line of work; do not imply APMG works only in childcare.

### Scheduled grounds maintenance (contract / PPM model)
APMG runs recurring scheduled ("fixed scope") grounds-maintenance visits, documented via formal Scope of Works, Job Sheets, and before/after (PPM) photo reporting. Scheduled scope shown includes:
- Mowing and edging (grass cut with a catcher; clippings removed; edges of gardens, paths, fence lines, mowing strips)
- Car park mechanical sweeping/blowing; clearing grated drains and pit inlets
- Playground/verandah/pergola/patio sweeping; returning sand to sandpits; removing debris
- Tree, shrub, and hedge trimming and pruning (with stated exclusions, e.g. pruning above 3 metres not included)
- Garden bed weeding and hedge pruning
- Fascia gutter and downpipe inspection/cleaning and pest-issue reporting (gutter cleaning above 3 metres excluded)

**Time & materials allocation (per Scope of Works, Page 10):**
- Each scheduled visit carries a **2-hour task allocation** for completing the fixed scope.
- The commitment is structured as **"2 x 2 hour visits"**, with **"4 hours per site must be fulfilled."**
- **If all fixed-scope tasks are completed early within a visit, the technician is to assist the Centre Director (CD) with any small handyman works** during the remaining allocated time.
- **Ancillary items** used in providing the service — fuel, rags, bags, waste removal — are **included in the fixed price**. **Additional materials are itemised on the monthly invoice** (with receipts retained for presentation on request).

Note: scheduled grounds attendance is described as weekdays within normal operating hours at an agreed day/time (weekend attendance excluded for the fixed scope).

### Sustainability practices (cross-service)
- Separating and responsibly disposing of construction/maintenance waste; working with recycling partners to reduce landfill
- Sourcing certified sustainable materials and low-VOC / eco-friendly paints where practical
- Electrical work prioritising LED upgrades, smart systems, and energy-efficient fittings
- Using locally based teams and subcontractors to reduce travel and vehicle emissions
- A make-safe / repair / maintenance-first approach to extend asset life and reduce premature replacement
- Installing/servicing water-efficient fixtures (dual-flush systems, flow restrictors, sustainable irrigation)

## Sector-specific needs / pain points APMG addresses
- **Child and staff safety** — hazard identification and rectification (trip hazards, finger guards, door closers, playground compliance).
- **Regulatory compliance** — works aligned to relevant regulations and compliance standards (emergency lighting/exit signage, playground safety assessments).
- **Minimal disruption** — working around centre schedules and daily routines to keep learning environments calm; fast, discreet delivery during operating hours.

- **Protecting brand standards** — high-quality finishes that support the centre's learning experience and brand image.
- **Reliability & responsiveness** — dependable, quick-to-respond service for both scheduled upkeep and urgent repairs.

(Treat this as an accreditation displayed, not as an audited guarantee that every individual staff member has been cleared — the source shows only the badge.)
- **Whole-of-site coverage through one contractor** — reducing the need to coordinate multiple trades/vendors.

## Guardrails for the email writer

**You MAY:**
- Reference any service or trade explicitly listed above (core trades, internal fit-outs, safety/compliance works, outdoor spaces, scheduled grounds maintenance, sustainability practices).
- Cite the accreditations/memberships shown (Paintback, Dulux Accredited Painter, HIA Member, MPA, Working with Children Check, Cm3, SAM4 Schools). - Reference the structured grounds-maintenance model (scheduled fixed-scope visits, documented via Scope of Works / Job Sheets / before-and-after PPM photos; ancillary items included in the fixed price with extra materials itemised on the monthly invoice) — useful for multi-site operators wanting predictable, accountable upkeep.
- Lean on APMG's positioning: one reliable multi-trade partner, early-learning specialisation, minimal disruption, safety/compliance-first, and care-centred delivery.
- Use the verbatim brand phrases above to keep tone consistent.
- Note that APMG uses a documented, four-stage process with formal scopes, job sheets, and before/after reporting.

**You MUST NOT:**
- Invent or imply any statistic, metric, or figure (years operating, number of centres, response times, % ratings, staff count).
- Claim any guarantee, warranty, insurance detail, license number, or certification not listed here.
- State or imply that every APMG staff member has been individually Working with Children Check-cleared — the source shows the WWCC only as an accreditation badge, so keep it to "carries the Working with Children Check accreditation."
- Imply APMG works exclusively in early learning — its mission statement also lists **industrial, commercial, and residential clients**. Early learning is a specialisation, not its sole market.
- Claim 24/7 or emergency/after-hours service — the source says "urgent repairs" and "quick to respond" but states scheduled attendance is weekdays within normal operating hours; do not promise round-the-clock availability.
- Claim national coverage or a specific service radius — the only stated location is the Chirnside Park, VIC base and "locally based teams"; do not assert a geographic footprint the source doesn't state.
- Introduce services APMG hasn't listed (e.g. asbestos removal, fire-system certification, security monitoring, HVAC design) — if it isn't in this KB, don't mention it.
- Overstate the client relationships: the named brands are shown as existing clients on APMG's portfolio; reference them tactfully as "trusted by leading early learning providers" and avoid implying endorsements, exclusivity, or claims those clients haven't made.

**Always tailor to the recipient:**

- Keep APMG's official name as **Australian Property Maintenance Group / APMG Services** (some source imagery is ambiguous — one small hoodie reading appears as "Australian Painting Maintenance Group" — but "Property" is the name used on the cover, logo, and mission statement, so use it).
