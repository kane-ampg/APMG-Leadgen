## What APMG is

APMG Services (Australian Property Maintenance Group) is a Melbourne-based,
family-run **multi-trade property maintenance company**, founded in 2015 by
Farbod Mollaei. It grew from a small painting operation into an end-to-end
property maintenance provider for facility managers, councils, body corporates,
and businesses across Melbourne and Victoria.

- Tagline: **"More Than Maintenance. We're Your Property Partner."**
- Positioning: **"End-to-end property maintenance solutions"** delivered by
  **"licensed, multi-trade professionals"** under one roof.
- Vision: *"To be the most trusted and versatile property maintenance partner,
  setting the benchmark for quality, reliability, and customer care across every
  trade we deliver."*

## ⚠️ What APMG is NOT — read before writing any email

APMG **maintains and repairs the recipient's buildings and grounds.** It is **NOT**
a lead-generation, marketing, or customer-acquisition agency.

**Never write** anything like:
- "we connect you with families/clients searching for your services"
- "a steady stream of qualified, ready-to-buy customers / leads"
- "fill your schedule", "more customers", "revenue you're missing out on"
- "help you get found / show up when people search"

That framing is wrong for APMG and must never appear. APMG sells **trade and
maintenance services to the facility** (painting, electrical, plumbing, grounds,
safety works, make-safe, etc.) — it does not bring the facility new customers.

The correct angle: APMG keeps the recipient's site **safe, compliant, well-
maintained, and running smoothly**, across every trade, through one reliable
partner — with minimal disruption to residents / children / students / staff.
